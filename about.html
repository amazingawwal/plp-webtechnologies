About Us

Founded in 2015, GreenField Poultry Farm is a family-run business dedicated to providing fresh, high-quality poultry products to our community.

Our Mission

We believe in sustainable, ethical farming practices. Our chickens are raised in open spaces, fed natural diets, and cared for with respect.

Why Choose Us?

  • Farm-fresh eggs collected daily
  • Healthy and antibiotic-free chickens
  • Commitment to eco-friendly farming
  • Trusted by local families and restaurants
